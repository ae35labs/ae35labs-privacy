Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 04, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the policies of ae35.labs regarding the Stay Tracker Europe mobile application (the "Service"). This app is a 100% free utility designed with "Privacy by Design" principles.

1. Data Collection & Multi-User Profiles

We do not collect "Personal Data" or PII. The Service allows for the creation of multiple user profiles (e.g., for family members), but all such data is handled as follows:

  • Local Storage Only: All profile names and travel dates are stored only on your physical device. We cannot see who is using the app or what their travel history is.
  • No External Transmission: Profile data is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud services.

2. App Permissions

  • Location: Used locally for calculation assistance if enabled. No location history is transmitted or stored by ae35.labs.

3. Compliance (UK, EU, & USA)

We comply with the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and US State Privacy Laws (CCPA/CPRA). Because we do not collect, sell, or share data, your statutory rights are maintained by your ability to manage or delete data within the app at any time.
