Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 04, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures of ae35.labs ("the Company", "We", "Us", or "Our") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information within the Schengen Days mobile application (the "Service").

Privacy by Design: Schengen Days is a "Zero-Data" application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal travel data to any external servers. Your data stays on your device.

1. Data Collection & Processing

We do not collect "Personal Data" or "Personally Identifiable Information" (PII) as defined by the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and US State Privacy Laws (CCPA/CPRA, VCDPA, etc.).

  • No User Accounts: No registration, email address, or phone number is required to use the Service.
  • On-Device Storage: All entry/exit dates and visa calculations are stored locally on your mobile device. We have no access to this data.
  • No Third-Party Trackers: We do not use invasive analytics, advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID), or trackers to profile users.

2. App Permissions (Consent-Based)

The Application may request the following permission to enable core features:

  • Location Services: If granted, location data is used locally to assist with border-crossing calculations. This information is processed in real-time on your device and is never sent to our servers.

3. US State Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

For residents of California, Virginia, Colorado, and other US states with active privacy legislation:

  • No Sale or Sharing: We do not "sell" or "share" your personal information (as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA).
  • Right to Know & Delete: Since we do not collect your data, we do not hold information to "delete" or "disclose." You maintain full control by managing data within the App or uninstalling it.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

4. UK & EU GDPR Compliance

In accordance with the UK and EU General Data Protection Regulations:

  • Lawful Basis: Local processing is based on Contractual Necessity (to provide the requested calculations) and Consent (for system permissions).
  • Data Controller: ae35.labs serves as the Data Controller.
  • Data Portability: Users may view and manage all locally stored data through the App interface.

5. International Transfers

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no international transfers of your personal information to third countries or international organizations.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any data from children. We comply with the **COPPA (USA)** and the **UK Age Appropriate Design Code** by ensuring no data collection occurs for any user, regardless of age.
