Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 04, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ae35.labs ("the Company", "We", "Us", or "Our") handles information within the Schengen Family mobile application (the "Service"). This policy is designed to meet the requirements of both the UK Data Protection Act 2018 (UK GDPR) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Controller: For the purposes of data protection laws, ae35.labs is the Data Controller. Because our Service is designed with "Privacy by Design" principles, we do not transmit or store your personal identifiers on our infrastructure.

1. Data Collection & Processing

The Service is a Zero-Data Collection application. We operate on the principle that your travel data belongs to you, and you alone.

  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or government IDs. No account registration is required.
  • Local-Only Storage: All travel dates, logs, and visa calculations are stored exclusively on your physical device. This data is never uploaded to our servers.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use advertising IDs (IDFA/AAID) or invasive analytics to profile your behavior.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

Under EU and UK law, we must specify the legal basis for any data processing. To the extent that the App processes data locally on your device, the legal basis is:

  • Contractual Necessity: Local processing is required to provide the visa calculation services you requested.
  • Consent: Any optional device permissions (such as Location) are processed only after you provide explicit consent via system prompts.

3. Device Permissions

To provide specific features, the Application may request:

  • Location Data: Used locally to assist in tracking border crossings. This information is processed in real-time on your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

4. International Data Transfers

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted to our servers, no international transfer of personal data occurs. Your data remains within your control on your device, regardless of your geographic location.

5. Your Rights (UK & EU)

Under the GDPR (EU) and UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since your data is stored locally:

  • Access & Portability: You can view all data within the App interface.
  • Erasure: You can delete all data at any time by clearing the App cache or uninstalling the Application.
  • Lodging a Complaint: You have the right to complain to a supervisory authority (such as the ICO in the UK or your local Data Protection Authority in the EU).

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not collect information from anyone. We do not knowingly process data from children under 16 (EU) or 13 (UK). Our Service is safe for general audiences as it lacks data-harvesting capabilities.
