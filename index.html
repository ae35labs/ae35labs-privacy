Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 04, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ae35.labs ("the Company", "We", "Us") handles information within the Schengen Family application (the "Service"). Your privacy is our priority, and this document outlines our commitment to a "Zero-Data" collection approach.

Our Privacy Philosophy: We believe that your travel data is your business. Schengen Family is designed to function without requiring, collecting, or storing any of your Personal Data on our servers.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect "Personal Data"—which is defined as information that can identify you. Our app is built on the following pillars:

  • No Accounts: You are not required to create an account or provide an email address to use the Service.
  • Local Storage: All travel dates, history, and visa calculations are stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party advertising trackers, analytics, or profiling tools that identify you or your specific device.

2. App Permissions

The Application may request certain permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Location Services: If enabled, this is used solely to determine your current border status for real-time calculations. This data is processed locally and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your data on any external servers, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised via a server-side data breach. You are responsible for the security of your own device. We recommend using device-level security such as Passcodes, FaceID, or TouchID.

4. UK GDPR & Statutory Rights

We strictly adhere to the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025. Because we do not process your data on our infrastructure:

  • Right to Access/Erasure: You can exercise these rights instantly by managing the data within the app settings or by deleting the Application from your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect or request any information from children under the age of 13. In accordance with the UK Age Appropriate Design Code, our data processing is designed with the best interests of the user in mind.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
