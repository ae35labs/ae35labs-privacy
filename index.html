Privacy Policy (Zero-Data Collection) Last updated: April 04, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how ae35.labs ("the Company", "We", "Us") handles information within the Schengen Family application (the "Service"). 1. Our Privacy Philosophy We believe that your travel data is your business. Schengen Family is designed to function without requiring, collecting, or storing any of your Personal Data. 2. No Personal Data Collection We do not collect any "Personal Data" (information that can identify you). No Accounts: You do not need to create an account or provide an email address to use the app. No Cloud Storage: All travel dates and calculations are stored locally on your device. We cannot see, access, or recover your data. No Tracking: We do not use third-party advertising trackers, analytics, or profiling tools that identify you or your device. 3. Permissions (Optional) The app may request the following permission for core functionality: Location Services: If enabled, this is used only to determine your current border status for calculations. This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. 4. Data Security Because we do not collect your data, there is no risk of your personal information being breached from our servers. Your data stays under your physical control on your mobile device. We recommend using your device’s built-in security features (Passcode, FaceID) to protect the information you enter into the app. 5. UK GDPR & Your Rights Although we do not hold your data, we strictly adhere to the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025. Because we do not store your data, "Right to Access" or "Right to Erasure" requests are usually unnecessary—you can delete your data at any time simply by deleting the app or clearing the app data in your settings. 6. Children’s Privacy Our Service is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any identifiable information, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children under 13. 7.
